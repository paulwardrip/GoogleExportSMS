Google SMS Exporter

With this tool you can merge the text messages from your Android device with backups from Google Voice and/or Hangouts, so you can import all of your messages into the SMS app of your choice.

  • Install SMS Backup & Restore
  • Backup your SMS messages to Dropbox or Google Drive
  • Go to Google Takeout.
    • Click Select None to clear the options you won't need.
    • Select the app(s) you want to export messages from Hangouts, Voice (or both).
    • Also select Contacts.
    • Proceed to export your data.
  • Enter your full name
  • Enter your phone #
  • Drag and Drop the most recent sms-yyyymmddhhmmss.xml from SMS Backup & Restore
