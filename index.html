Google SMS Exporter

If you have used Google Voice or Hangouts and you have switched or want to switch to a different SMS app on your Android phone, then this app is for you.

All the SMS messages you have sent using Google Voice / Hangouts are available online and you can go back into those apps to read your old messages, but they aren't stored as SMS messages in your phone, so you can't read them in another text messaging app... but what if you could?

Tell me more!

This website and the free Android app SMS Backup & Restore will allow you to import those messages back into your phone as SMS and then you can use whatever text messaging app you want and have your full message history.

While this application will ask you for personal information and files, we do not keep any of this information! The work all happens in your browser, no data is ever sent to the server hosting this page, it never leaves your computer!

Step 1

Install SMS Backup and Restore on your phone.

It's installed!

Step 2

  • Use SMS Backup & Restore to backup the SMS messages on your phone to Dropbox or Google Drive
  • Wait for the file to synchronize to your local filesystem (or download it if you are not using that feature of your chosen storage system).
  • Drag and drop your sms-yyyymmddhhmmss.xml file directly onto this window.
I don't want to import from my phone.

Step 3

Go to Google Takeout.
  • Click Select None to clear the options you won't need.
  • Select the app(s) you want to export messages from
    Hangouts, Voice (or both).
  • Also select Contacts.
  • Proceed to export your data. Unzip any zip files you received and proceed to the next step.
I have my data, proceed.

Step 4

  • Enter your full name
  • Enter your phone #

It is important that both of those fields are correctly entered, please be sure not to make a mistake.

In your takeout files locate All Contacts.vcf

Drag and Drop it into this window.

This step cannot be skipped.

Step 5

Drag and Drop your Hangouts.json into this window.

I'm not importing anything from Hangouts.

Step 6

Drag and Drop your whole Calls folder from Voice into this window.

I'm not importing anything from Voice.
